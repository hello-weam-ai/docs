# 🤝 Welcome to Weam

> **The AI boom is here—but adoption is messy.**  
> Teams are overwhelmed with too many tools, scattered workflows, and unclear strategies. Whether you’re a solo founder or leading an innovation team at a Fortune 500 company, the biggest challenge isn’t access to AI—it’s **using it effectively, securely, and collaboratively**.

---

## 🧠 Open-Source AI for Real-World Teams

**Weam is an open-source AI platform** that helps teams adopt, customize, and scale AI workflows. From structured prompt libraries to fully trained agents and secure workspaces, Weam brings everything into one collaborative hub—so your team can move from idea to execution with confidence.

Use it in the cloud or host it yourself—**no black-box tools, no vendor lock-in**.

---

## 🌟 Why Choose Weam?

Weam combines the flexibility of open-source with the structure modern teams need to thrive.

### 🔑 Key Capabilities

- 💡 **Multi-Model Support** – Use GPT-4, Claude, and more side-by-side.
- 🧠 **Brains** – Keep project memory and context cleanly scoped.
- 🛠 **Custom AI Agents** – Build smart, task-specific agents on your own data.
- ⚡️ **Power Prompts** – Expand short inputs into ready-to-run prompts.
- 🔄 **Fork & Share Conversations** – Seamless collaboration across users and teams.
- 📚 **Prompt & Agent Libraries** – Use proven templates or create your own.
- 📂 **Import Chat Histories** – Bring in conversations from OpenAI or Anthropic.
- 🔐 **Secure, Role-Based Access** – Built for team control and enterprise scale.
- 🔓 **Open-Source Core** – Fully extensible and customizable by your dev team.
