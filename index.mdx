**Weam is an open-source platform** designed to supercharge how digital teams adopt and integrate AI in their workflows. Whether you're a startup agency or an established enterprise, Weam gives you full control over your AI interactions—from chat-based brainstorming to enterprise-level automation.

This guide will help you unlock the full potential of Weam so your team can collaborate smarter, automate faster, and innovate with confidence.

---

### 🌟 Why Weam?

At its core, **Weam simplifies the complex world of AI**—making it accessible, scalable, and tailored for team collaboration. With built-in support for multiple AI models, secure data handling, and powerful tools for managing prompts, agents, and conversations, Weam helps you focus on what really matters: getting things done.

Here’s a quick glimpse of what makes Weam different:

- 💡 **Multi-LLM Switcher** – Use GPT-4, Claude, and more in one workspace.
- 🧠 **Brains** – Organize projects and teams with clean separation of data and context.
- 📂 **Import Chats** – Bring in your entire chat history from OpenAI or Anthropic in seconds.
- 🛠 **Custom AI Agents** – Build task-specific agents trained on your own data.
- ⚡️ **Power Prompts** – Turn short instructions into detailed prompts with a click.
- 🔄 **Fork & Share Conversations** – Collaborate seamlessly with teammates or clients.
- 🔐 **Secure Workspaces** – Manage permissions, roles, and access at scale.
- 📚 **Prompt & Agent Libraries** – Start instantly with curated templates for marketing, sales, HR, and more.
